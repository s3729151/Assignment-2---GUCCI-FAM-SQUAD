Interest in IT

My interest in the IT field was started when I was doing my year 10. This developed to a great high when Mr.Sundar Pichai became he CEO of Google in 2015. His story inspired me and that made me to choose IT as my carrier.

Dream University

My main objective of this scavenge is to find a dream university in which I could get experimental and realistic command of an IT company. My Expectation

The main objective that I expect from the university is to gain practical knowledge and hands on practice. I would expect to learn some soft skills which is required to fulfil my job conditions. Some important soft skills such as time management where we prioritise our task on daily basis. Problem solving skills is very important skill which requires in all the parts of an IT sector. Teamwork is one of the important and a necessary skill where most of the IT projects are done in a team. Adaptability is a skill which is a part of the teamwork where we should adapt with the team members of different character and culture.
