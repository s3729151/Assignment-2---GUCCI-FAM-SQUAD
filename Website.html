PROJECT IDEA


FILL ME IN

Growing up, during my free time I would always walk down to my local basketball court and play basketball there. Most of the time I would be there playing alone but sometimes some stranger would come along and join in and it was always a great time and experience to play against different people. My idea to resolve the problem of people around the world having to play sports alone, is to create an app that displays the closest parks or sporting centres near you and it would also act as a bit of a social network that would display if there are any people playing around you right now or what time they’ll be coming to play.

 

While talking about this app I will always be referring to Basketball as it’s what I play but keep in mind that my aim for this app is to have it available for as many different sports possible.

 

This idea will be successful as in my local community I know many people that play basketball and many more outside of my local community so just imagine how many people would use this app to stay connected to friends, team mates and even just random people that are down to play. Sports are always more fun when playing with other people or against each other, shooting around by yourself will never be more exciting and better than shooting around with others or even playing a classic 5v5. This idea also interests me as I was having a talk about it with my basketball team and they all said it would be a “game changer.”

 

The first time the user downloads the app and enters, the app will prompt them to create a profile where they can choose their chosen sport, add their username and password, add a biography, add the courts near them or the courts they frequently visit and also optionally add a schedule on when they’re usually free to play.  Once all of that is out of the way, there will also be an option if the user would like to connect their account to Facebook for an easier way to find friends that are already using the app. Once the whole registration part is finished the user will be free to use the app and access all of its features. When opening the app, the app will display a large map highlighting all of the basketball courts near the user and the highlight will be a different colour for different meanings, for example, a red highlight if there are more than 10 people at the court, a green highlight if there are 5 or more people on the court, a yellow highlight if there are 2 or more people on the court, a white highlight if there’s no one at the court and a blue highlight if there aren’t any people at the court for now but people are coming. When viewing the map, the user will also see kind of like a snapchat style location feature where they get to see where their friends are on the map. When a user selects a court, the information of the exact people that are there will be displayed, an option to invite friends to this court, the information about the court, (whether the court is a half or full court, what type of ring it is, if its indoor or outdoor, if there’s shade if it’s outdoor and if there’s any taps nearby. The user could also announce that they are at a court or they will be at a court to others that have that court saved as their local court. The user would also share their info on which court they will be at or which court they’re at by selecting the court, but this could also be done automatically, when the user walks onto a court the app could just gather that the users there using location services and just update in the app that the user is currently there. For now, that is the full app but some fun implementations could be added such as competitions at courts with a set date and time, a leader board that displays who’s spent the most time at a specific court which would label them the “king of the court” and also a rating and review system of different courts.

 

To create the app, the program xcode would be used to code the app, it is free in the apple app store so it is quite accessible and easy to install. To download xcode I would need a laptop, preferably an apple mac. I would also need to have a mobile phone to test what the app would look like in person from time to time. No special hardware, software or tools are required in the development of this app.

 

Having knowledge about coding using the swift language which is basically CC+ is a must and the developer should have past experience on developing apps using the swift language. No special hardware will be needed to create this app. There’s nothing too special about this app that would require any special equipment or talented hackers to create so I think the app could be created by a university student familiar with coding with swift.

 

If this app becomes successful, it would mean thousands of people are being connected with each other through sports and possibly becoming more active, the local sporting communities would be outside more, utilising the courts provided and creating a better image for local parks. People like me that would always play alone would finally have friends to play with and be more motivated to play the game.
